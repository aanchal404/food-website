Veg Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bread (white or brown)
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 small tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 small boiled potato, sliced
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Chaat masala or black pepper (optional)
  • Green chutney (optional)

Instructions:

Spread butter or mayonnaise on each bread slice. Optionally, apply green chutney on one side of each slice.

Place slices of cucumber, tomato, and potato on one slice. Sprinkle chaat masala or pepper for extra taste.

Cover with another slice. Cut diagonally and serve fresh with ketchup or chutney.
