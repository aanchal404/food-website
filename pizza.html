Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza base (thin crust)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F). Place the pizza base on a baking tray.

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese generously on top.

Add fresh basil leaves and drizzle olive oil over the pizza. Sprinkle a pinch of salt if desired.

Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until the cheese melts and turns slightly golden. Slice and serve hot!
