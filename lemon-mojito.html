Lemon Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lemon, cut into wedges
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 cup soda or sparkling water
  • Lemon slice and mint for garnish

Instructions:

In a glass, add lemon wedges, mint leaves, and sugar. Use a muddler or spoon to gently crush and mix to release flavors.

Fill the glass with ice. Pour soda or sparkling water over it. Stir gently and garnish with a lemon slice and mint leaves.

Serve chilled and enjoy your refreshing lemon mojito!
