Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

  • 250g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Thick Yogurt (Hung Curd)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Onion and Capsicum (sliced)
  • Skewers

Method:

In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and spices. Add paneer cubes and vegetables to this marinade and coat well. Let it rest for 30-60 minutes in the refrigerator.

Thread the marinated paneer and veggies onto skewers. Grill on a pan or in an oven at 200°C for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until lightly charred and cooked through.

Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges. Enjoy your homemade Paneer Tikka!
