Mushroom Chilli

Ingredients:

  • 200g Button Mushrooms (cleaned and halved)
  • 1 Medium Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tbsp All-purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying and cooking

Method:

Mix cornflour, all-purpose flour, salt, pepper, and water to make a thick batter. Coat mushrooms in the batter and deep fry until golden brown.

In a pan, heat 2 tbsp oil. Sauté onions, green chilies, and bell pepper until soft. Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, ketchup, and vinegar. Stir well.

Add the fried mushrooms, toss everything for 2-3 minutes on high heat. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot as an appetizer or side.
