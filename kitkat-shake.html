KitKat Shake

Ingredients:

  • 2 KitKat bars (regular size)
  • 2 cups chilled milk
  • 2 scoops vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • 2 tsp chocolate syrup
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Instructions:

Break the KitKat bars into pieces and add them to a blender.

Add chilled milk, ice cream, and chocolate syrup. Blend until smooth.

Pour the shake into a glass. Top with whipped cream and crushed KitKat if desired.

Serve immediately with a straw and enjoy your delicious KitKat shake!
