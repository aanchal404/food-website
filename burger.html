Veg Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 boiled potato, mashed
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter or oil for toasting
  • Lettuce or cabbage leaves
  • Tomato and onion slices
  • Mayonnaise or ketchup

Instructions:

Mix mashed potato, carrot, capsicum, pepper, and salt. Shape into patties and cook on a pan until golden brown.

Slice the buns and toast them with butter. Spread mayonnaise or ketchup on each side.

Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and the veggie patty. Cover with the top bun. Serve hot!
